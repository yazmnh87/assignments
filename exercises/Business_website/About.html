History

In 2015, in response to the student debt crisis and with a solid background in finance and education, Tom Pitcher began collaborating with a team of remarkable young people to find a solution – the Q3 Way! Q3 Student Partners launched a successful pilot program in the fall of 2016 with a cohort of first-generation students in Salt Lake City, Utah. Local partners now embrace the Q3 Way and are scaling it in West Valley City, Utah. Under its recently-launched When Life Happens fund, Q3 is expanding its reach to help college-bound students follow through with their education when the unthinkable happens.
